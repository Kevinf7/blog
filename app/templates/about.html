About Me

My name is Kevin and I have over 20 years of working experience in a variety of IT related areas including software development, web design and information management. The latter in the area of library and information science. I recently started learning Python, at first because I wanted to get into data science, but then found Flask along the way. I now consider myself to be a Python and Flask enthusiast and am interested in sharing my knowledge and learning from others.

This website was coded entirely from scratch by hand using the following technologies:

  • Python
  • Flask. Libraries include:
    • Flask-Login
    • Flask-SQLAlchemy
    • Flask-Mail
    • Flask-Migrate
    • Flask-Moment
    • Flask-WTF
  • mySQL
  • Bootstrap CSS

It includes admin features such as blog post management, managing tags and is also a mini content-management system, allowing you to manage various parts of the website without needing to go into the backend.
